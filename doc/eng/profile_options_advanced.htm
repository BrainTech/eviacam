FACE LOCALIZATION

 

 

- Locate face automatically

 

The user's face is automatically tracked to center the Active Zone box.

NOTE: Sometimes background colors similar to the skin can confuse eViacam. In case of trouble disable this option and center the box manually making double click on the user's nose.

 

- Show locate face filter

 

Show the color-space skin filter in real-time. Useful to detect skin similar color in the background.
